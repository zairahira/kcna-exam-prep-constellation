  points={[
    <><strong>Namespaces</strong> partition a cluster. Scope names, RBAC, and resource quotas.</>,
    <>Default namespaces: <code>default</code>, <code>kube-system</code>, <code>kube-public</code>, <code>kube-node-lease</code>.</>,
    <><strong>Namespaced</strong> resources: Pods, Deployments, Services, ConfigMaps, Secrets, PVCs. <strong>Cluster-scoped</strong>: Nodes, PVs, StorageClasses, ClusterRoles.</>,
  ]}

<Remember
  points={[
    <><strong>Labels</strong>: queryable key-value metadata. How controllers and Services find their objects.</>,
    <><strong>Selectors</strong>: equality-based (<code>env=prod</code>) or set-based (<code>tier in (a,b)</code>). Combine with commas = AND.</>,
    <><strong>Annotations</strong>: free-form metadata for humans and tools. Not queryable.</>,
  ]}
/>

<Remember
  points={[
    <><strong>ConfigMaps</strong>: non-sensitive config. <strong>Secrets</strong>: sensitive config (base64-encoded, not encrypted by default).</>,
    <>Consume as <strong>env vars</strong> (resolved at Pod start - need restart to update) or as <strong>mounted volumes</strong> (eventually refreshes).</>,
    <>Enable etcd encryption at rest for real Secret confidentiality.</>,
  ]}
/>

<Remember
  points={[
    <><strong>Ephemeral volumes</strong> (Pod-scoped): <code>emptyDir</code> (scratch), <code>configMap</code>/<code>secret</code> (projected config), <code>downwardAPI</code> (Pod metadata). <code>hostPath</code> is discouraged.</>,
    <>Container filesystems are ephemeral - use volumes to persist or share between containers in a Pod.</>,
  ]}
/>

<Remember
  points={[
    <><strong>PV</strong>: cluster-scoped piece of storage. <strong>PVC</strong>: namespaced request for storage. PVC binds to a PV that satisfies it.</>,
    <>Access modes: <strong>RWO</strong> (one node), <strong>ROX</strong> (many nodes, read-only), <strong>RWX</strong> (many nodes, read-write - needs networked storage), <strong>RWOP</strong> (one Pod).</>,
    <>Reclaim policy: <strong>Retain</strong> (keep data after PVC delete) or <strong>Delete</strong> (wipe). Pick per data sensitivity.</>,
  ]}
/>

<Remember
  points={[
    <>A <strong>StorageClass</strong> + <strong>provisioner</strong> enables dynamic PV creation when a PVC requests it.</>,
    <>A <strong>default StorageClass</strong> serves PVCs that don&apos;t specify one.</>,
    <><code>volumeBindingMode: WaitForFirstConsumer</code> defers provisioning until Pod scheduling - important for zonal storage.</>,
    <><strong>CSI</strong> (Container Storage Interface) is the standard for storage plugins. All modern integrations are CSI-based.</>,
  ]}
